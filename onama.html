O nama

Dear friends, hello, we are Home4you.me, a real estate boutique of exclusive properties.

We were founded within the group of companies according to our respected clients’ needs with the most experienced professionals in the real estate market, tourism and investment consulting in Montenegro.

The Home4you main priority is deeper understanding of the client’s needs and preferences which, together with our extensive investment experience, allows the client to feel secure and confident. We are a unique Montenegrin Group which has several separate business directions, from creation of the ideas and concepts of the property to its construction and implementation within dozen years of work and experience.

Our company takes care of every client, providing the perfectly tailor-made range services:

* Legal services accompanying purchase and sales transactions;

* Maintenance of the purchased properties;

* Tourism and travel-friendly services;

* Concierge, including and not limited by yacht and car rental;

* Investment consulting services;

* Business and legal consulting.

* Moreover we arrange 1-3 day free inspection tours across all Montenegrin coast of the Adriatic sea/North of Montenegro by special request.

What is included in such a trip:

1) Transfer airport-hotel-airport – FREE

2) Property observation trip across all coast – FREE

Every day after breakfast our property manager will pick you up from the hotel/apartment in order to see the properties, and after you finish the observation bring you back to the hotel.

The trip will include not just examination of properties, also slow walks to the beach and back, see infrastructure and surroundings and realize if you feel comfortable at the place.

3) Beaches and infrastructure observations – FREE;

4) all types of consulting on the way – FREE.

We have own Management company in the Group structure that will serve your monthly costs payments, take care of your property, rent it profitably without a visit to Montenegro.

It is important for us that each client is happy and fully informed of the country, where he is planning to buy property, that’s why we arrange individual scrupulous work with every our client.

Also, Home4you has a wide chain of partners in Balkan, Russia and Western Europe, having plans to increase globally nevermind pandemia times and open accredited representative offices in the Russian Federation and special project offices/franchisee worldwide.
